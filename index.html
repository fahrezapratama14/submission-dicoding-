Who is Roselia?

Roselia is an all-girl band in the BanG Dream! franchise. The group consists of five members, namely Minato Yukina on vocals, Hikawa Sayo on guitar, Udagawa Ako on drums, Shirokane Rinko on the keyboard, and Imai Lisa on bass. They debuted with the song BLACK SHOUT. Their band name was coined from combining the words Rose and Camellia. They're a popular gothic J-Rock band that is recognized at a near-professional level and is catching the eyes of the music industry and scouters.BanG Dream, also known as Bandori , is a Japanese music media franchise owned by Bushiroad. Created by Bushiroad president Takaaki Kidani in January 2015 with an original story by Ko Nakamura, the project began as a manga before expanding to other media. In addition to several other manga adaptations, the franchise consists of an anime series adaptation, live concerts, a number of music.
